The OIML SMART pilot programme is now live in development. This site is the
working draft of the public-facing documentation, deployed for the internal
use of the OIML SMART team and OIML Member State reviewers.

## What's in scope

Three OIML International Recommendations are being modelled in the pilot:

- **R 60** — Metrological regulation for load cells
- **R 129** — Dynamic measurement of road vehicle mass
- **R 144** — Diaphragm gas meters

For each, the pilot produces:

- Machine-readable **requirements** with structured acceptance criteria
- **Conformance tests** with explicit cross-references back to requirements
- Calculation-powered **test report forms**
- A **domain ontology** in OWL
- End-to-end certification workflow through OIML-CS

## What this site is

This site is **for the internal use of the OIML SMART team**. It is part of
the pilot programme and every page, document, and specification on it is a
**draft** that may change without notice.

OIML Member States and Corresponding Members seeking engagement should
contact OIML through official channels. This site is not approved for
external distribution.

## What's next

Over the coming months the pilot will:

1. Complete the modelled entities for R 60, R 129, R 144.
2. Run internal conformance tests against representative data.
3. Refine the OIML-CS workflow integration.
4. Publish a public-facing summary at a future milestone.
