## What's available

The OIML Vocabularies site hosts **12 terminology datasets** covering the full historical range of OIML vocabulary publications:

### VIM — International Vocabulary of Metrology (OIML V 2 series)

The foundational vocabulary for measurement science, jointly maintained with BIPM, IEC, IFCC, ILAC, ISO, IUPAC, and UIPAP.

| Edition | Reference | Concepts |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| 1984 | OIML V 2:1984 | 138 |
| 1984 (corrigendum) | OIML V 2:1984/Cor.1:1987 | 138 |
| 1993 | OIML V 2:1993 | 120 |
| 2007 | OIML V 2-200:2007 | 144 |
| 2010 | OIML V 2-200:2010 | 144 |
| 2012 | OIML V 2-200:2012 | 144 |

### VIML — International Vocabulary of Legal Metrology (OIML V 1 series)

The OIML-specific vocabulary extending VIM with legal metrology concepts: legal control, type approval, verification, statutory responsibilities.

| Edition | Reference | Concepts |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| 1968 | OIML V 1:1968 | 276 |
| 2000 | OIML V 1:2000 | 44 |
| 2013 | OIML V 1:2013 | 135 |
| 2022 (current) | OIML V 1:2022 | 135 |

### G 18 — Term-Usage Registry

A usage registry (not a vocabulary) collating every instance where a term is used across OIML Recommendations and Documents, with each instance's definition and a consistency check against the VIM/VIML definition.

| Edition | Reference | Terms |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| 2010 (published) | OIML G 18:2010 | 2,125 |
| 202X (draft) | OIML G 18 Edition 202X | 2,814 |

## What the browser offers

Built with the [Glossarist Concept Browser](https://github.com/glossarist/concept-browser), the site provides:

- **Cross-edition browsing** — compare how a concept's definition evolved across editions
- **Full-text search** within each dataset
- **Cross-reference navigation** — follow term relationships between concepts
- **Bilingual content** — English and French terminology side-by-side

## Relationship to SMART

In the OIML SMART pilot, the vocabulary provides the semantic foundation for machine-readable Recommendations:

- **Consistent terminology** across all modeled Recommendations
- **Concept identifiers** that can be referenced from structured requirements
- **Definition provenance** linking terms back to the authoritative source

The vocabulary data is maintained in [Glossarist](https://github.com/glossarist) YAML format in the [oimlsmart/vocab](https://github.com/oimlsmart/vocab) repository.
