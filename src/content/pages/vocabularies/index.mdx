## What are OIML Vocabularies?

The **International Vocabulary of Legal Metrology (VIML)**, published by OIML as **OIML V 1:2022**, defines the standardized terminology used across all OIML International Recommendations and Documents. It is the authoritative reference for terms in the field of legal metrology.

The VIML aligns with the **International Vocabulary of Metrology (VIM)** — the broader vocabulary for measurement science — extending it with concepts specific to legal metrology such as legal control, type approval, verification, and statutory responsibilities.

## What the browser offers

The OIML Vocabularies site is a statically-deployable terminology browser built with the [Glossarist Concept Browser](https://github.com/glossarist/concept-browser). It provides:

- **Full-text search** across all 135 concepts and their definitions
- **Cross-reference navigation** — follow term relationships between concepts
- **Bilingual content** — English and French terminology side-by-side
- **Authoritative definitions** sourced from OIML V 1:2022

## Relationship to SMART

In the OIML SMART pilot, the vocabulary provides the semantic foundation for machine-readable Recommendations:

- **Consistent terminology** across all modeled Recommendations
- **Concept identifiers** that can be referenced from structured requirements
- **Definition provenance** linking terms back to the authoritative source

The vocabulary data is maintained in [Glossarist](https://github.com/glossarist) YAML format in the [oimlsmart/vocab](https://github.com/oimlsmart/vocab) repository.
