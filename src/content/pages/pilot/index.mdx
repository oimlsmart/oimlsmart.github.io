## What this means

Everything on this site — every requirement, test, form, ontology entity, and specification — is part of the **OIML SMART pilot programme** and is a **DRAFT**.

This means:

- **Content may change without notice.** As the pilot evolves, requirements may be added, removed, or restructured. Test procedures may be updated. Form schemas may be modified. Do not rely on any specific detail remaining stable.
- **Not for external distribution.** The pilot is shared with OIML Member States and Corresponding Members for internal review. It has not been formally adopted or published by OIML.
- **Models are working drafts.** The SMART models of R 60, R 129, and R 144 demonstrate the approach but are not the authoritative versions of these Recommendations. The authoritative source remains the published OIML PDF documents.

## What the pilot is exploring

The pilot tests whether OIML Recommendations can be expressed as **machine-actionable** structured artifacts rather than static PDFs. Key questions:

- Can requirements, tests, and forms be modeled with enough precision that compliance can be computed, not just read?
- Can the structured data drive an interactive application for OIML-CS participants (Issuing Authorities, Test Laboratories, Manufacturers)?
- Can a shared terminology (VIM/VIML) provide semantic consistency across Recommendations?
- Is the YAML-based authoring workflow practical for OIML Technical Committees?

## Timeline and scope

The pilot began in 2024 and is ongoing. Three Recommendations are currently modelled:

| Recommendation | Scope | Status |
|---|---|---|
| OIML R 60 (Load cells) | Most complete model — requirements, tests, forms, calculations | Active development |
| OIML R 129 (Mass of road vehicles) | Partial model — requirements and tests | In progress |
| OIML R 144 (Gas meters) | Partial model — requirements | In progress |

The pilot also includes:
- A [terminology browser](/vocabularies/) covering 12 VIM/VIML/G 18 datasets
- An [interactive application](/app/) demonstrating the dispatch, evaluation, and certification workflow
- A [developer documentation](/docs/) suite covering the architecture, guides, and formal specifications
