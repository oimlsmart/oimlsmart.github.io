## Publication

- **Code:** R 144
- **Edition:** 2006 (E)
- **Published by:** Organisation Internationale de Métrologie Légale (OIML)
- **Custodian:** OIML Technical Committee **TC 8** (Measurement of quantities of fluids)

## Scope

This Recommendation specifies metrological and technical requirements for
**diaphragm gas meters** — positive-displacement instruments that measure
fuel gas volume by alternately filling and emptying chambers formed by
flexible diaphragms.

It covers:

- Residential and commercial meters from **G1.6** to **G100** (rated flow capacity in m³/h).
- Accuracy classes **1.0**, **1.5**, and **2.5**.
- Meters operating on fuel gases at pressures up to 0.5 bar (low pressure) or 200 mbar (typical service).

## Accuracy classes

| Class | MPE in upper zone (Qt to Qmax) | MPE in lower zone (Qmin to Qt) | Typical use |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1.0** | ±1.0% | ±3.0% | Custody transfer (high precision) |
| **1.5** | ±1.5% | ±3.0% | Custody transfer (standard) |
| **2.5** | ±2.5% | ±5.0% | Industrial sub-metering |

The transition flow `Qt` is declared per meter size and class. The
two-zone MPE structure is encoded as a Table primitive in the SMART model.
