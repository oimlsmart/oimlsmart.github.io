## What it covers

B 18 establishes:

- The **scope** of the OIML-CS — which categories of measuring instruments are covered.
- The **roles** — Issuing Authorities (IAs), Test Laboratories (TLs), Utilizers, Associates, Applicants/Manufacturers.
- The two **schemes**: **Scheme A** (accreditation/peer assessment based) and **Scheme B** (self-declaration based).
- The **Declaration** mechanism by which Member States and Corresponding Members commit to participation.
- The **Management Committee** that governs the OIML-CS.
- The relationship to the BIML (Bureau International de Métrologie Légale).

## SMART connection

The OIML-CS is the operational system that uses SMART International
Recommendations to issue digital type examination certificates.
