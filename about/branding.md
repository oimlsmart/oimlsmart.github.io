## Type

The OIML SMART type system uses three families:

- **Source Serif 4** — serif headings and display type.
- **IBM Plex Sans** — body text, navigation, and UI.
- **IBM Plex Mono** — code, data, monospaced labels (URNs, identifiers).

## Usage guidelines

- The SMART logo must always be displayed with adequate clear space (at least the height of the globe mark on all sides).
- The Brand 600 blue is the primary color. Teal and amber are reserved for accents.
- On dark backgrounds, always use the `-dark` logo variant. Never place a light variant on a dark background.
- The OIML-CS logo is reserved for content directly related to the OIML Certification System. Do not use it for general SMART programme content.
- Do not stretch, recolor, or rearrange the logo elements. Maintain the original aspect ratio.

For questions about logo usage not covered here, contact BIML via [www.oiml.org](https://www.oiml.org).
