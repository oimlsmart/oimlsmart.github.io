## OIML BIML

The Bureau International de Métrologie Légale (BIML) is the secretariat of the OIML, responsible for coordinating the organization's activities and supporting member states in the development and implementation of OIML Recommendations.

- **Address:** 11 Rue Turgot, 75009 Paris, France
- **Website:** [www.oiml.org](https://www.oiml.org)

## Get Involved

The OIML SMART programme is developed in collaboration with OIML member states, technical committees, and the broader metrology community. Here's how to participate:

- **OIML Member States** — participate in Technical Committees and Subcommittees developing SMART International Recommendations.
- **Standards Developers** — use the developer guides and specification to make new standards SMART-compatible.
- **OIML-CS Participants** — Issuing Authorities, Test Laboratories, and Manufacturers can use the digital certification tools.
- **Tool Vendors** — integrate SMART deliverables into conformity assessment, laboratory management, and certificate registry software.

## Technical enquiries

For technical questions about the SMART platform, the Primmel model, or the published specifications, file an issue on the relevant [GitHub repository under the `oimlsmart` org](https://github.com/oimlsmart). Pull requests are welcome.
